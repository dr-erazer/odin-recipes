Biryani

Description

Chicken Biryani is a fragrant and flavorful dish that combines marinated chicken with aromatic basmati rice. The chicken is tenderized in a blend of yogurt and spices, while the rice is partially cooked with fragrant spices. These elements are then layered in a pot with fried onions, saffron-infused milk, and fresh herbs. The dish is slow-cooked to perfection, allowing the flavors to meld together. Chicken Biryani offers a delightful mix of textures and tastes, making it a beloved and satisfying Indian rice dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb (450g) chicken pieces
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 1 tsp each ginger and garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • A few cardamom pods, cloves, and cinnamon
  • Salt
  • 2 large onions, thinly sliced and fried
  • Saffron strands soaked in warm milk
  • Fresh cilantro and mint

Instructions

  1. Marinate chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, spices, and salt. Chill for 2 hours.
  2. Parboil basmati rice with spices and drain when 70-80% cooked.
  3. Fry onions until golden and crispy.
  4. Layer chicken, rice, fried onions, and herbs in a pot.
  5. Drizzle saffron milk on top for color and aroma.
  6. Cover and cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes.
  7. Fluff and serve hot with raita or salad.
