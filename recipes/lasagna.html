Lasagna

Description

Lasagna, a beloved Italian classic, is a dish that harmoniously layers lasagna noodles with indulgent combinations of meaty sauces, creamy cheese fillings, and vibrant tomato sauces. This culinary creation is carefully assembled and then baked to a golden perfection, resulting in a flavorful and comforting casserole that's sure to delight the taste buds of anyone fortunate enough to partake in its delicious layers.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb (450g) ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 28 oz crushed tomatoes
  • 14 oz tomato sauce
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp each dried basil and oregano
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp chopped parsley
  • 12 no-boil lasagna noodles
  • 2 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Instructions

  1. Sauté chopped onions and minced garlic in a large skillet. Add ground beef and cook until browned.
  2. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, basil, oregano, salt, and black pepper. Simmer for 20-30 minutes.
  3. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, grated Parmesan, egg, chopped parsley, salt, and black pepper.
  4. Layer meat sauce, lasagna noodles, cheese filling, and mozzarella in a baking dish.
  5. Repeat the layers and top with mozzarella.
  6. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 25 minutes covered, then 15-20 minutes uncovered until golden and bubbly.
  7. Rest for 15 minutes before serving. Enjoy your delicious homemade lasagna!
